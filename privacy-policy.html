Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use X Shadowban Checker, we collect the following information:

  • X/Twitter username — You voluntarily enter a username to check its shadowban status.
  • Anonymous usage data — We collect basic page visit statistics (page URL, referrer, timestamp) for analytics purposes. This data is anonymized and does not identify individual users.

2. How We Use Your Information

The X/Twitter username you enter is sent to a third-party API (shadowban-api.yuzurisa.com) to perform the shadowban check. We do not store, log, or share the usernames you check.

Anonymous visit statistics are used solely to understand overall site usage and improve the service.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Shadowban API (shadowban-api.yuzurisa.com) — Performs the actual shadowban check. Username data is sent to this API temporarily.
  • Google AdSense — Displays advertisements. Google may use cookies and collect data as described in their privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare — Provides CDN and security services.
  • GitHub Pages — Hosts this website.

4. Cookies

This site uses a cookie to remember your language preference (English or Chinese). Google AdSense may use cookies for personalized advertising.

5. Data Storage

Anonymous visit statistics are stored temporarily and are not linked to any personal information. We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide the service.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the email address associated with our domain registration.

7. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
